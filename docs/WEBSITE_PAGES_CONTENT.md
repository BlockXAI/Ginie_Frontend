### Our Story

Ginie was born from a simple observation: building smart contracts shouldn't require months of learning Solidity, understanding complex deployment pipelines, or navigating the intricate world of blockchain infrastructure.

We set out to create a tool that empowers developers of all skill levels to bring their blockchain ideas to life — quickly, securely, and without the traditional barriers to entry.

Today, Ginie serves developers, startups, and enterprises who want to move faster in the Web3 space without compromising on security or quality.

---

### What We Do

**AI-Powered Generation**
Describe your smart contract in plain English, and our AI generates production-ready Solidity code. No more starting from scratch or copying boilerplate.

**One-Click Deployment**
Deploy to multiple blockchain networks with a single click. We handle the complexity of gas estimation, network configuration, and transaction management.

**Wallet-Based Deployments**
Connect your own wallet for full control over deployments. Your keys, your contracts, your ownership.

**Project Management**
Track all your deployments, manage contract versions, and access your deployment history from a unified dashboard.

---

### Our Values

**Simplicity First**
We believe powerful tools don't have to be complicated. Every feature we build prioritizes ease of use.

**Security by Default**
Smart contracts handle real value. We integrate security best practices into every generated contract and provide comprehensive audit trails.

**Developer Empowerment**
We're not here to replace developers — we're here to amplify what they can accomplish. Ginie handles the tedious work so you can focus on innovation.

**Transparency**
Open pricing, clear documentation, and honest communication. No hidden fees or surprise limitations.

### Introduction

Ginie ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Please read this policy carefully. By using Ginie, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

### Information We Collect

**1. Information You Provide**

- **Account Information:** Email address, display name, and profile details when you create an account.
- **Wallet Information:** Public wallet addresses you connect for deployments. We never have access to your private keys.
- **Communications:** Information you provide when contacting support or providing feedback.
- **Payment Information:** If you subscribe to Pro, payment details are processed by our secure payment processor. We do not store full payment card details.

**2. Information Collected Automatically**

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, deployment history, and interaction patterns.
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and IP address.
- **Cookies:** We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, preferences, and analytics.

**3. Information from Third Parties**

- **Blockchain Data:** Publicly available transaction data related to your deployments.
- **Analytics Providers:** Aggregated usage statistics from analytics services.

---

### How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our services
- Process transactions and send related information
- Send technical notices, updates, and support messages
- Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests
- Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
- Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity
- Personalize and improve your experience
- Comply with legal obligations

---

### Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in these circumstances:

**Service Providers**
With third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, payment processing).

**Legal Requirements**
When required by law, legal process, or government request.

**Business Transfers**
In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

**With Your Consent**
When you have given us explicit permission to share.

**Aggregated Data**
We may share aggregated, non-identifiable statistics about platform usage.

---

### Blockchain Considerations

**Public Nature of Blockchain**
When you deploy smart contracts through Ginie, transaction data is recorded on public blockchains. This data is immutable and publicly accessible. We cannot delete or modify blockchain records.

**Wallet Addresses**
Your public wallet address and associated transactions are visible on blockchain explorers. Do not share sensitive information through smart contracts.

---

### Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and authentication requirements
- Secure development practices

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at privacy@Ginie.app.

## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Design Consistency
- Use existing color scheme (black background, orange accents, white text)
- Maintain current typography (Inter, Space Grotesk fonts)
- Apply `page-transition` class for smooth animations
- Use existing UI components (Button, Card, etc.)

### SEO Considerations
- Add appropriate meta titles and descriptions
- Use semantic HTML headings (H1, H2, H3)
- Include structured data where applicable
- Add canonical URLs

### Accessibility
- Ensure proper heading hierarchy
- Add alt text for all images
- Maintain sufficient color contrast
- Make all interactive elements keyboard accessible

### Mobile Responsiveness
- Stack layouts appropriately on mobile
- Ensure readable font sizes
- Touch-friendly button/link sizes
- Collapsible sections for long content (Privacy Policy)
